About Us

Kettle Bliss was founded in 2023 to bring elegance, quality, and joy to the art of tea brewing. Our products are crafted with the utmost attention to detail to ensure each piece is a work of art in your home.

Our Journey

  • 2023: Kettle Bliss is founded and launches its first collection.
  • 2024: Introduces sustainable packaging and expands product line.
  • 2025: Opens flagship showroom in Tea Town, CA.

Our Mission

At Kettle Bliss, we believe that tea should be enjoyed slowly, mindfully, and in style. Our mission is to create products that enhance the tea ritual and bring timeless beauty to everyday life.
